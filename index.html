01 ABOUT

MY SANCTUARY.

ZACH WILMOT

Trainer Portrait

I'm Zach Wilmot — a personal trainer with over nine years of experience in the fitness industry, based in Seattle's Lower Queen Anne.

Having spent the last few years working with a startup during the COVID-19 pandemic with minimal availability for personal training clients, I'm happy to open my books to new clients again.

Practicing self-love, discipline, and dedication has transformed my life. My passion lies in creating an environment that enhances individuals' quality of life through fitness — and I pride myself on going above and beyond for every client.

My approach focuses on injury prevention, eliminating the guesswork from your gym routine, and providing personalized guidance to help you achieve your fitness goals.
